Update IV for next sample. block_offset_ is reset to 0. As recommended in ISO/IEC FDIS 23001-7: CENC spec, For 64-bit IV size, new_iv = old_iv + 1; For 128-bit IV size, new_iv = old_iv + previous_sample_block_count.
